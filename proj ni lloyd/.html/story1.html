The Golden Touch

Author: Nathaniel Hawthorne

Characters:

  • King Midas - A greedy king
  • Dionysus - A god who grants wishes
  • Princess - Midas' beloved daughter

Story:

King Midas loved gold more than anything. One day, he was granted a wish: everything he touched would turn to gold.

Excited, he touched his furniture, food, and even flowers—all turned into gold. But when he hugged his daughter, she turned into a golden statue.

Realizing his mistake, Midas begged for the curse to be undone. He learned that love is more valuable than wealth.
