The Honest Woodcutter

Author: Aesop

Characters:

  • Woodcutter - A poor but honest man
  • River Spirit - A magical being who tests honesty

Story:

A woodcutter accidentally dropped his axe into a river. The River Spirit appeared and offered him a golden axe, but the woodcutter said it wasn’t his.

Then, the spirit showed him a silver axe, but he refused again. Finally, she gave him his real axe. Seeing his honesty, the spirit rewarded him with all three axes.

Moral: Honesty is always rewarded.
