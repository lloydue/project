The Little Red Hen

Author: Florence White Williams

Characters:

  • Little Red Hen - Hardworking and wise
  • Lazy Cat, Dog, and Duck - Unwilling to help

Story:

The Little Red Hen found wheat and asked for help planting it. The cat, dog, and duck refused.

She harvested, ground the wheat, and baked the bread—all alone. When the bread was ready, the animals wanted some, but she said, "Since you didn’t help, you won’t eat."

Moral: Those who do the work deserve the reward.
