The Three Little Pigs

Author: James Orchard Halliwell-Phillipps

Characters:

  • First Pig - Built a house of straw
  • Second Pig - Built a house of sticks
  • Third Pig - Built a house of bricks
  • Big Bad Wolf - The villain

Story:

Three little pigs built their own houses. The first pig used straw, the second used sticks, and the third used bricks.

The Big Bad Wolf blew down the straw and stick houses, but he couldn't destroy the brick house.

The third pig saved his brothers, proving that hard work and preparation lead to safety.
