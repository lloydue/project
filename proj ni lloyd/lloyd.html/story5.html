The Ugly Duckling

Author: Hans Christian Andersen

Characters:

  • Ugly Duckling - A misunderstood bird
  • Farm Animals - Bullied the duckling
  • Swans - Showed the duckling his true identity

Story:

A little duckling was born different. He was teased for being ugly and left alone. After struggling through a harsh winter, he found a group of swans.

Looking at his reflection, he realized he had grown into a beautiful swan. He was never ugly—he was just different.

Moral: True beauty comes from within, and differences make us special.
